Our Acidity Relief Tea is a premium loose-leaf herbal infusion crafted from carefully selected botanicals traditionally enjoyed to support digestive comfort and everyday wellbeing. Naturally caffeine free and prepared using premium loose herbs, it is designed to complement a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle.

Product Information

  • Net Weight: 60 g

Ingredients

  • Anantmool – Traditionally valued in Ayurvedic herbal wellness.
  • Guduchi – One of Ayurveda's most respected botanicals.
  • Bala – Traditionally appreciated in herbal preparations.
  • Rasna – A classic Ayurvedic botanical.
  • Ginger – Traditionally enjoyed to support digestive comfort.
  • Shatavari – A well-known herb in Ayurvedic traditions.
  • Coriander – A fragrant seed commonly used in herbal infusions.
  • Liquorice – Traditionally valued for its soothing properties.
  • Green Cardamom – An aromatic spice appreciated for generations.
  • Milk Thistle – Traditionally included in herbal wellness blends.
  • Marshmallow – Traditionally enjoyed in botanical infusions.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 5–10 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Drink twice daily after meals.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
