Our Anti Diabetic Tea is a premium loose-leaf herbal infusion crafted from a thoughtfully selected blend of botanicals traditionally valued in herbal wellness practices.   • Bitter Melon – Traditionally enjoyed as part of healthy living.

  • Senna – Traditionally included in herbal preparations.
  • Fenugreek – A popular herb in traditional wellness systems.
  • Guduchi – Traditionally appreciated in Ayurveda.
  • Jamun Seed – Long valued in traditional herbal use.

  • Milk Thistle – Traditionally associated with liver wellness.

  • Ashwagandha – A well-known adaptogenic herb in Ayurveda.

  • Clove – A fragrant botanical long used in traditional herbal preparations.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 5–10 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Drink twice daily after meals, or as advised by your healthcare professional.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
