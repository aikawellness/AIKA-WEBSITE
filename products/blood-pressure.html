Blood Pressure Balance Tea

Our Blood Pressure Balance Tea is a premium loose-leaf herbal infusion crafted from a carefully selected blend of botanicals traditionally valued in herbal wellness. Naturally caffeine-free and prepared using premium loose herbs, it is designed to complement a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • Tulsi – Traditionally respected in Ayurveda.
  • Gotu Kola – A well-known botanical used in traditional wellness practices.
  • Flax Seed – A naturally nutrient-rich seed enjoyed in many healthy diets.
  • Hibiscus – Popular in refreshing herbal infusions.
  • Cardamom – An aromatic spice appreciated for generations.
  • Cinnamon – A warming spice widely used in herbal traditions.
  • Juniper Berry – Traditionally used in botanical preparations.
  • Punarnava – Long valued in Ayurveda.
  • Gokhru – Traditionally included in herbal formulations.
  • Ashwagandha – A renowned adaptogenic herb.
  • Jatamansi – Traditionally appreciated in Ayurvedic herbal practices.
  • Arjuna – One of Ayurveda's best-known heart-supporting botanicals.
  • Burdock – Traditionally included in herbal wellness blends.
  • Nettle – A nutrient-rich herb enjoyed in traditional herbal teas.
  • Lemon Balm – Valued for its pleasant aroma and calming character.
  • Brahmi – Traditionally respected for overall wellbeing.
  • White Mulberry Leaf – A botanical long used in traditional herbal preparations.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 5–10 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Drink twice daily after meals, or as advised by your healthcare professional.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
