Blue Tea

Our Blue Tea is a premium loose-leaf herbal infusion made from carefully selected butterfly pea flowers. Naturally caffeine free and known for its beautiful deep blue colour, this elegant herbal tea is a refreshing addition to your daily wellness routine.

Product Information

  • Net Weight: 60 g
  • Approximately: 30 Servings
  • Type: Premium Loose Leaf Herbal Infusion
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free

Ingredients

  • Butterfly Pea Flowers (Blue Pea) – Naturally rich in plant antioxidants and traditionally enjoyed as a vibrant herbal infusion.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 3–5 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Enjoy hot or serve over ice for a refreshing cold herbal tea.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
