Our CCF Digestive Tea is a premium loose-leaf herbal infusion inspired by the traditional Ayurvedic combination of Cumin, Coriander and Fennel. Naturally caffeine free and crafted using premium herbs, it is designed to complement a balanced diet and everyday digestive wellness.

Product Information

  • Net Weight: 60 g
  • Approximately: 30 Servings
  • Type: Premium Loose Leaf Herbal Infusion
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free

Ingredients

  • Cumin – Traditionally enjoyed after meals in Ayurvedic wellness.
  • Coriander Seed – A fragrant botanical valued in traditional herbal preparations.
  • Fennel – Long appreciated for its refreshing flavour and digestive traditions.
  • Triphala – A classic Ayurvedic blend of three fruits respected for centuries.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 5–10 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Drink after meals or as part of your daily wellness routine.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
