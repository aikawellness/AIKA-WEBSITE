Ingredients

  • Turmeric – A celebrated botanical in traditional herbal wellness.
  • Ginger – Traditionally enjoyed as part of everyday wellbeing.
  • Tulsi – Revered in Ayurveda for its long history of traditional use.
  • Fenugreek – A classic herb in traditional wellness systems.
  • Coriander – A fragrant seed widely used in herbal infusions.

  • Amla – Naturally rich in plant antioxidants.
  • Cinnamon – A warming spice enjoyed around the world.
  • Arjuna – One of Ayurveda's best-known botanicals.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 5–10 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Drink twice daily after meals.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
