PREMIUM FLOWERING TEA

Blooming Tea Balls

Blooming Tea Balls are hand-tied artisan tea blossoms crafted from premium green tea leaves and delicate jasmine, lily and osmanthus flowers. As hot water is added, each tea ball slowly unfurls into a beautiful floral display, creating a memorable tea experience that combines elegance, visual theatre and refined flavour—perfect for luxury hospitality settings.

Ingredients

  • Premium Green Tea
  • Jasmine Flowers
  • Lily Flowers
  • Osmanthus Flowers

Preparation

  • Place one Blooming Tea Ball into a clear glass teapot or large glass cup.
  • Add 400–500 ml of hot water (80–85°C).
  • Allow the tea ball to slowly bloom over 3–5 minutes.
  • Serve immediately to showcase the beautiful floral display.
  • The same tea ball may be infused 2–3 times.
