Blue Pea

Blue Pea (Butterfly Pea Flower) is one of the world's most captivating botanical infusions, admired for its naturally vibrant blue colour and elegant presentation. Rich in naturally occurring anthocyanins and antioxidants, it creates an unforgettable tea experience while adding visual appeal to mocktails, wellness drinks and premium hospitality menus.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Butterfly Pea Flowers
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Colour: Naturally Brilliant Blue
  • Presentation: Loose Floral Infusion
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Cafés, Restaurants & Wellness Centres

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Rich in naturally occurring anthocyanins and antioxidants.
  • Traditionally enjoyed to support overall wellbeing.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Creates an attractive wellness beverage with no artificial colouring.
  • Provides a memorable botanical tea experience for guests.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Butterfly Pea Flowers (Clitoria ternatea)

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 3–5 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • For a refreshing beverage, serve over ice.
  • Add lemon to create a beautiful natural colour transformation from blue to purple.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
