HOSPITALITY FLORAL INFUSION

Chamomile

Chamomile is a premium floral infusion treasured for its delicate golden colour, gentle floral aroma and naturally soothing character. Traditionally enjoyed as a calming herbal tea, it offers guests a relaxing and comforting experience, making it an excellent addition to hotels, spas, cafés and wellness retreats.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Whole Chamomile Flowers
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Colour: Golden Yellow
  • Presentation: Loose Floral Infusion
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Cafés, Restaurants & Wellness Centres

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Traditionally enjoyed for its naturally calming properties.
  • Delicate floral aroma and smooth taste.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Ideal for evening relaxation and wellness rituals.
  • Creates a comforting premium tea experience for guests.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Whole Chamomile Flowers (Matricaria chamomilla)

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (90°C).
  • Steep for 5–7 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • Best enjoyed in the evening or as part of a relaxing wellness ritual.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
