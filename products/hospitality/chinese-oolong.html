PREMIUM LOOSE LEAF TEA

Chinese Oolong

Chinese Oolong is a premium semi-oxidised loose leaf tea celebrated for its smooth, complex flavour and delicate floral aroma. Carefully crafted using traditional methods, it offers guests a refined tea experience that perfectly complements luxury hotels, fine dining restaurants, executive lounges and premium cafés.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Loose Leaf Oolong Tea
  • Main Ingredient: Chinese Oolong Tea Leaves
  • Caffeine: Naturally Contains Caffeine
  • Flavour: Smooth, Floral & Lightly Roasted
  • Presentation: Loose Leaf Tea
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Restaurants, Cafés & Executive Lounges

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Naturally rich in tea polyphenols and antioxidants.
  • Smooth, balanced flavour with floral notes.
  • Premium loose leaf tea for discerning guests.
  • Can be infused multiple times.
  • Offers an authentic Asian tea experience.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Chinese Oolong Tea Leaves

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2–3 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (85–90°C).
  • Steep for 3–5 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • The same leaves can be infused several times, revealing different flavour notes with each infusion.
