HOSPITALITY FLORAL INFUSION

Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemum is a premium floral infusion appreciated for its delicate golden blossoms, naturally refreshing taste and elegant floral aroma. Traditionally enjoyed as a soothing herbal beverage, it offers a refined tea experience that perfectly complements luxury hotels, spas, cafés and wellness retreats.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Whole Chrysanthemum Flowers
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Colour: Golden Yellow
  • Presentation: Loose Whole Flowers
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Cafés, Restaurants & Wellness Centres

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Traditionally enjoyed as a refreshing floral infusion.
  • Delicate floral aroma with a naturally smooth taste.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Provides an elegant botanical tea experience.
  • Ideal for wellness-focused hospitality menus.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Whole Chrysanthemum Flowers

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (85–90°C).
  • Steep for 3–5 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • For a refreshing summer beverage, serve over ice.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
