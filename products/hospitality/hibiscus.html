HOSPITALITY FLORAL INFUSION

Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a premium floral infusion renowned for its vibrant ruby-red colour, refreshing naturally tangy flavour and elegant presentation. Rich in naturally occurring antioxidants and naturally caffeine free, it creates a memorable tea experience whether served hot or iced, making it an excellent choice for luxury hotels, spas, cafés and restaurants.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Hibiscus Flowers
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Colour: Deep Ruby Red
  • Presentation: Loose Floral Infusion
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Cafés, Restaurants & Wellness Centres

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Naturally rich in antioxidants.
  • Refreshing floral infusion with a pleasantly tangy flavour.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Perfect served hot or as an iced botanical beverage.
  • Creates an elegant wellness tea experience for guests.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Hibiscus Flowers (Hibiscus sabdariffa)

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (90°C).
  • Steep for 5–7 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • For a refreshing beverage, serve over ice.
  • Sweeten with honey if desired.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
