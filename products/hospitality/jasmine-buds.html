Jasmine Buds

Jasmine Buds is a premium floral infusion crafted from carefully selected whole jasmine buds. Renowned for its delicate floral fragrance and naturally soothing character, it delivers an elegant tea experience that enhances luxury hotels, boutique resorts, spas and premium hospitality environments.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Whole Jasmine Buds
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Flavour: Delicate Floral Aroma
  • Presentation: Loose Whole Flower Buds
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Cafés, Restaurants & Wellness Centres

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Delicate naturally floral aroma.
  • Traditionally enjoyed as a calming botanical infusion.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Creates a luxurious guest tea experience.
  • Perfect for premium hospitality tea menus.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Whole Jasmine Buds

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (85–90°C).
  • Steep for 3–5 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • Excellent as a relaxing afternoon or evening floral infusion.
  • The same buds can be infused a second time.
