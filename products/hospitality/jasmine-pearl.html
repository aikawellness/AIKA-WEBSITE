PREMIUM LOOSE LEAF TEA

Jasmine Pearl

Jasmine Pearl is an exceptional handcrafted green tea made from young tea leaves delicately hand-rolled into pearls and naturally scented with fresh jasmine blossoms. This elegant tea offers a smooth flavour, exquisite floral aroma and a luxurious tea experience ideal for premium hospitality environments.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Jasmine Green Tea
  • Main Ingredient: Hand-Rolled Jasmine Pearl Tea
  • Caffeine: Naturally Contains Caffeine
  • Flavour: Smooth, Floral & Delicate
  • Presentation: Hand-Rolled Tea Pearls
  • Suitable For: Luxury Hotels, Restaurants, Executive Lounges & Premium Cafés

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Naturally rich in tea antioxidants.
  • Elegant floral aroma from natural jasmine blossoms.
  • Premium handcrafted loose leaf tea.
  • Can be infused multiple times.
  • Creates a memorable luxury tea experience.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Jasmine Pearl Green Tea

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2–3 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–85°C).
  • Steep for 3–4 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • The same pearls can be infused several times, revealing new layers of flavour.
