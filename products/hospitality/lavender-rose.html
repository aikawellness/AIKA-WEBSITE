Lavender Rose Tea

Lavender Rose Tea is a luxurious botanical blend combining fragrant lavender flowers with delicate rose petals. Carefully crafted to create a soothing floral infusion, this elegant tea delivers a refined aroma and a beautifully balanced flavour, making it ideal for luxury hotels, spas, wellness retreats and premium afternoon tea experiences.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Herbal Infusion
  • Main Ingredients: Lavender Flowers & Rose Petals
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Flavour: Delicate Floral with Sweet Rose Notes
  • Presentation: Loose Floral Blend
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Wellness Centres, Cafés & Restaurants

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Traditionally enjoyed for relaxation and calm.
  • Beautiful floral aroma with a smooth, delicate taste.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Creates an elegant wellness tea experience.
  • Perfect for premium hospitality tea menus.

Ingredients

  • Premium Lavender Flowers
  • Premium Rose Petals

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (90°C).
  • Steep for 5–7 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • Ideal for afternoon tea, spa rituals and evening relaxation.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
