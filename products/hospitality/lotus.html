HOSPITALITY FLORAL INFUSION

Lotus

Lotus is a premium floral infusion admired for its delicate fragrance, elegant appearance and naturally smooth taste. Traditionally cherished in many cultures, lotus flowers create a refined botanical tea experience that perfectly complements luxury hotels, wellness retreats, spas, fine dining restaurants and premium cafés.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Premium Lotus Flowers
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Flavour: Delicate Floral & Smooth
  • Presentation: Loose Whole Flowers
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Wellness Centres, Cafés & Restaurants

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Traditionally enjoyed as a calming botanical infusion.
  • Delicate floral aroma with a smooth, elegant taste.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Creates a luxurious tea experience for guests.
  • Ideal for wellness-focused hospitality menus.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Lotus Flowers

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (85–90°C).
  • Steep for 4–6 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • Also delicious served chilled as a refreshing botanical beverage.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
