HOSPITALITY FLORAL INFUSION

Rhododendron

Rhododendron is a distinctive premium floral infusion traditionally enjoyed in the Himalayan region for its refreshing flavour, delicate floral aroma and vibrant natural colour. Its unique botanical profile offers guests a memorable tea experience, making it an exceptional addition to luxury hotels, resorts, spas and premium cafés.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Premium Rhododendron Flowers
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Flavour: Light Floral with Refreshing Notes
  • Presentation: Loose Whole Flowers
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Resorts, Spas, Cafés & Restaurants

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Traditionally enjoyed as a refreshing botanical infusion.
  • Delicate floral aroma and naturally smooth taste.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Creates a unique premium guest tea experience.
  • Ideal for wellness-inspired hospitality menus.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Rhododendron Flowers

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (90°C).
  • Steep for 5–7 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • May also be served chilled for a refreshing botanical beverage.
  • The same flowers can be infused a second time.
