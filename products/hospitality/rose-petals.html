HOSPITALITY FLORAL INFUSION

Rose Petals

Rose Petals is a luxurious floral infusion crafted from premium edible rose petals, celebrated for its captivating aroma, delicate floral flavour and elegant presentation. This sophisticated botanical tea creates a memorable guest experience, making it a perfect choice for luxury hotels, spas, boutique cafés and fine dining establishments.

Product Information

  • Product Type: Premium Floral Infusion
  • Main Ingredient: Premium Rose Petals
  • Caffeine: Naturally Caffeine Free
  • Flavour: Sweet Floral & Fragrant
  • Presentation: Loose Rose Petals
  • Suitable For: Hotels, Spas, Cafés, Restaurants & Wellness Centres

Health & Wellness Benefits

  • Delicate floral aroma with a naturally smooth taste.
  • Traditionally enjoyed as a calming botanical infusion.
  • Naturally caffeine free.
  • Creates an elegant luxury tea experience.
  • Perfect for premium hospitality tea service.

Ingredients

  • 100% Premium Edible Rose Petals

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (85–90°C).
  • Steep for 4–6 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy hot.
  • Excellent served warm or chilled as a fragrant botanical beverage.
  • The same petals can be infused a second time.
