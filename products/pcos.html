PCOS Tea

Our PCOS Tea is a premium loose-leaf herbal infusion crafted from carefully selected botanicals traditionally valued in women's wellness. Naturally caffeine-free and prepared using premium loose herbs, it is designed to complement a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • Spearmint – Traditionally enjoyed in herbal wellness.
  • Chasteberry – Long valued in traditional herbal practices for women's wellbeing.
  • Ashwagandha – A renowned adaptogenic herb in Ayurveda.
  • St. John's Wort – Traditionally included in herbal preparations.
  • Nettle – A nutrient-rich botanical enjoyed in herbal infusions.
  • Gokshura – Traditionally respected in Ayurvedic wellness.
  • Flax Seed – Naturally rich in plant nutrients.
  • Shatavari – One of Ayurveda's best-known herbs for women's wellness.
  • Fenugreek – A classic botanical in traditional herbal systems.
  • Cinnamon – A warming spice widely appreciated in herbal traditions.
  • Nimbu Tulsi – A fragrant basil traditionally enjoyed in herbal infusions.
  • Liquorice – Traditionally valued for its soothing properties.
  • Guduchi – One of Ayurveda's most respected botanicals.
  • Blue Pea Flower – Naturally rich in plant antioxidants and appreciated for its vibrant colour.
  • Piper Longum – A classic Ayurvedic botanical.

Preparation

  • Use 1 teaspoon (approximately 2 g) per cup.
  • Add 250–300 ml of hot water (80–90°C).
  • Steep for 5–10 minutes.
  • Strain and enjoy.
  • Drink twice daily after meals, or as advised by your healthcare professional.
  • The same herbs can be reused for a second infusion.
